Privacy Policy for Simple Loan Calculator

Effective date: 2026-02-27

Simple Loan Calculator ("the App") is designed to work fully on your device.

Financial Disclaimer

The App is for informational and educational purposes only and does not provide financial, legal, tax, or investment advice. Any calculations are estimates only and should not be treated as professional advice.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data through the App. The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or cloud sync.

Data Stored on Your Device

To provide core functionality, the App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Loan input values (amount borrowed, interest rate, loan length, fee values)
  • Calculation preferences (currency and repayment options)
  • Saved loan profiles you choose to store in the App

This data is stored only on your device using local storage and is not sent to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your in-app loan data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App is built with Expo and React Native libraries. Some platform-level services (such as Google Play or device OS services) may process technical data according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for device-level protections (such as screen lock, device encryption, and backups).

Your Choices

You can delete your saved loan profiles directly in the App at any time. You can also uninstall the App to remove local app data from your device (subject to device backup settings).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will include a new effective date.
